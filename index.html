About Precious Gems School

WHAT WE HOPE TO OFFER TOO, IS A STYLE

Not easy to define, but there nonetheless and not particularly bookish, but academically vigorous, thoughtful and intelligent.

Competitive certainly, as befits a school such as ISSA, but relaxed and good-humoured independent of spirit. Hardly arty, but capable of surprising with the passion of its music, art and drama.

Strongly collegiate, with an easy and respectful relationship between teacher and child, between seniors and juniors, and between parents, staff and managementFor all their diversity subsequent to leaving school, and for all their individuality and independence, Precious Gem School tend to recognize each other as having been shaped by the same institution.
